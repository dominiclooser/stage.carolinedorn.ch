About

I am working at the University of Zurich as a Ambizione Fellow. I am associated with the Swiss NCCR PlanetS platform.

My research is funded by the Swiss National Science Foundation.

Education

I studied geophysics at the University of Leipzig, where I also did my minors in numerics and geology. My thesis was conducted at ETH Zurich.

At the University of Lausanne, I did my PhD in geophysics and environmental sciences, which was awarded with the faculty prize.
