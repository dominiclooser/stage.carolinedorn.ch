Exoplanets

Image: Thibaut Roger

There is a hunger for knowing whether we are alone in the Universe. This curiosity drives the exoplanet community to characterize planets outside our Solar system in order to get a picture of other worlds.

Astronomers have made tremendous achievements to find and characterize exoplanets using a wide variety of methods. The complexity of exoplanet interior characteri- zation demands interdisciplinary collaboration in order for the field to reach its potential role in characterizing planets. My research advances the characterization of both individual planets and planet distributions.
